Design Narrative

This essay reflects some of my personal beliefs surrounding design and attempts to accurately define what design means. I believe that design is the process of evolving our tools so that they harmonize with our actions. In the narrative I analyze several cases of intelligent design and highlight how good design strengthens the human race, allowing us to not only survive but thrive amongst other species. By improving and designing our surroundings we are constantly improving the quality of human life.
