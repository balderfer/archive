Design Glossary

In an attempt to better my understanding of design principles I sought out to create a glossary containing my own definitions of several principles. I chose hierarchy, contrast, tension, alignment, and motion because I believe that in order to create a beautiful design, all of those principles must be in play. To help further my understanding of each principle I turned to various resources online which helped evolve my definitions into their final form. Along with simply delivering the definitions I also employed each principle within the glossary itself.
