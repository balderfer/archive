Infographic

Through the evaluation of Purdue University's online registration tool, I sought out to create a visually compelling infographic that helps guide first time users through the process of registering for classes. In the first iteration of the infographic I learned how to best structure the document in order to guide the reader through the steps. After peer review, I chose to wrap the infographic in a style closely following the Purdue brand guidelines.
