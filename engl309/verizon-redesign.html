Verizon Redesign

Three classmates and I revisited the core of Verizon's brand, their logo. After identifying the needs of the business and the purpose of the brand, we attempted to redesign the logo. One of the main issues we saw with Verizon's current logo is the lack of personality. While it was following the trend of simplification, it lost much of the brand's unique style. While adhering to red checkmark and the logotype format, we feel that we have created a powerful new logotype for the company.
