Cyber Sustainability

A key goal of the expansion is to create a robust collaboration between Computer Science and the College of Agriculture to bring the power of Big Data to improve agricultural productivity. In particular, there are plans to create a Center for Cybersustainability that will bring together researchers and industry from both fields and foster the developing the next generation of advances that will help feed a growing planet and contribute to the entrepreneurial enterprise in Indiana.

Fostering exploration and entrepreneurship through the creation of an Innovation lab where students can experiment with various hardware and software platform and test their inventions. A space for this lab has been identified and plans for equipment are being finalized.
