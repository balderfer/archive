Global Experience in Computer Science

The Global Experience in the College of Science provides students with the opportunity to take classes, conduct research, pursue an internship, engage in service learning, and to be immersed in another culture. The following international Computer Science programs are designed for Purdue CS students to gain exposure to global perspectives on Computer Science and to take courses not available on Purdue’s campus. These three pre-articulated programs are available to all qualifying CS juniors and seniors, and are designed to keep students on track to obtain their degree.

Tsinghua University
Beijing, China

Learn all about cryptography for designing secure systems on Tsinghau University’s campus, which is situated on the site of a royal garden of the Qing dynasty.

Website

University of Canterbury
Christchurch, New Zealand

Steep yourself in security concepts for stored and transmitted information in the “garden city” of New Zealand.

Website

University of New South Wales
Sydney, Australia

Explore network protocols at one of Australia’s great universities.
