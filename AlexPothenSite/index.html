About

Alex's research interests are in combinatorial scientific computing (CSC), high performance computing, and bioinformatics. CSC is an interdisciplinary research area where discrete mathematics and algorithms are applied to combinatorial problems from Computational Science and Engineering. CSC links scientific computing to algorithmic computer science.

Current projects include: Graph algorithms on parallel computers; Computing sparse Hessians via Automatic Differentiation; Real-time matrix computations for simulating surgery; Algorithms for aligning massive networks via relaxations of quadratic integer programs; and Algorithms for analyzing, registering, and classifying Flow Cytometry (proteomic) samples.
