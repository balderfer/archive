Practice Areas

  • Business Services

    We represent corporate, governmental and other types of organizations that engage in a wide variety of business or commercial activities. Our lawyers are skilled in formation, capitalization and other financing matters, and provide advice on contractual, regulatory, employment, tax, property, and other business-related matters.

    Learn More

  • Environmental Law

    Our team has considerable expertise in compliance and enforcement issues involving federal and state environmental laws. We also assist clients in obtaining permits and approvals from regulatory agencies such as the Indiana Department of Environmental Management and the Indiana Department of Natural Resources.

    Learn More

  • Estate Planning

    The estate planning and administration group represents individual and corporate fiduciaries in connection with trust, estate and guardianship administrations. We also assist clients with retirement planning, charitable giving, preserving and transferring business interests, minimizing taxes and preparing powers of attorney.

    Learn More

  • Health Care

    Our firm represents individual physicians, practice groups, and institutional health care providers in the areas of practice management, health care transactions, contract review, business organization, and issues involving organ donation and procurement.

    Learn More

  • Lending

    Firm attorneys with a focus in lending practices represent clients with equity financing arrangements, such as partnerships, joint ventures and syndications and both borrowers and lenders with structuring and documenting letters of credit, revenue bonds, stand-by credit, real estate construction and mortgage loans and personal property lending.

    Learn More

  • Litigation

    Our litigation attorneys represent clients in trial and appellate matters regarding contractual, real estate, regulatory, employment, property, tax and other business-related disputes. We also represent insurance companies in actions involving coverage issues, as well as first-party claims and suits against insureds.

    Learn More

  • Pipelines

    Attorneys in the firm have assisted interstate natural gas, petroleum and other types of pipelines with a wide variety of issues related to securing rights to install pipelines across the State of Indiana, including regulatory permitting and compliance and right-of-way acquisition through the use of eminent domain.

    Learn More

  • Real Estate

    We provide counsel on matters such as property acquisition and disposition, development, leasing, zoning and land use, historic preservation, and environmental regulation for owners, brokers, developers, landlords, tenants and government agencies.

    Learn More

  • State and Local Taxation

    We represent clients with local, regional and national legal matters related to real property and business personal property taxes, as well as income and sales taxes imposed by the State of Indiana.

    Learn More

  • Utilities

    Our utility attorneys handle regulatory matters for electric, gas, water and sewer utilities before the Indiana Utility Regulatory Commission, Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, and other regulatory agencies. Our team also counsels clients on issues related to broadband and other communications services.

    Learn More
