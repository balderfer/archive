    Lante Earnest concentrates his practice in the areas of business law (both transactions and organization), employment and commercial law, housing, government/administrative law, estate planning and administration, and real estate law. Mr. Earnest received the highest rating for legal ability by Martindale Hubbell, and has been selected for inclusion in the listing of Indiana Super Lawyers® for the years 2004 through 2011. Distinctions include: Recipient, 1989, Spirit of Philanthropy Award from I.U.P.U.I., 1994, I.U. School of Law-Indianapolis Distinguished Alumni Award. Member of the Board of Directors of Heritage Trust and Indiana Natural Resources Foundation, 2003-2005. Mr. Earnest is a Fellow of the Indianapolis Bar Foundation (Board of Directors, 2000-2005; President, 2003); Indiana Bar Foundation.
