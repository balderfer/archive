    Timothy K. Ryan is a partner concentrating primarily in the areas of real estate, business services and estate planning/probate administration. Since joining the firm in 1980, he also has been actively involved in all aspects of litigation matters and has served as personal representative or trustee of numerous complex estates and trusts.

    Mr. Ryan is a member of the Indianapolis Bar Association as well as the Indiana State Bar Association. He is also a member of Omicron Delta Kappa.
